class QuadgramAssocMeasures(NgramAssocMeasures): (source)

A collection of quadgram association measures. Each association measure is provided as a function with five arguments:: trigram_score_fn(n_iiii, (n_iiix, n_iixi, n_ixii, n_xiii), (n_iixx, n_ixix, n_ixxi, n_xixi, n_xxii, n_xiix), (n_ixxx, n_xixx, n_xxix, n_xxxi), n_all) The arguments constitute the marginals of a contingency table, counting the occurrences of particular events in a corpus. The letter i in the suffix refers to the appearance of the word in question, while x indicates the appearance of any word. Thus, for example: n_iiii counts (w1, w2, w3, w4), i.e. the quadgram being scored n_ixxi counts (w1, *, *, w4) n_xxxx counts (*, *, *, *), i.e. any quadgram
