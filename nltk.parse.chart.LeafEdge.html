class LeafEdge(EdgeI): (source)

Known subclasses: nltk.parse.pchart.ProbabilisticLeafEdge

View In Hierarchy

An edge that records the fact that a leaf value is consistent with a word in the sentence. A leaf edge consists of: - An index, indicating the position of the word. - A leaf, specifying the word's content. A leaf edge's left-hand side is its leaf value, and its right hand side is ``()``. Its span is ``[index, index+1]``, and its dot position is ``0``.
