@staticmethod
def multiply(discourse, readings): (source)

Multiply every thread in ``discourse`` by every reading in ``readings``. Given discourse = [['A'], ['B']], readings = ['a', 'b', 'c'] , returns [['A', 'a'], ['A', 'b'], ['A', 'c'], ['B', 'a'], ['B', 'b'], ['B', 'c']] :param discourse: the current list of readings :type discourse: list of lists :param readings: an additional list of readings :type readings: list(Expression) :rtype: A list of lists
