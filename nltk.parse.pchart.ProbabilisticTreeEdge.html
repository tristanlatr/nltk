Construct a new ``TreeEdge``. :type span: tuple(int, int) :param span: A tuple ``(s, e)``, where ``tokens[s:e]`` is the portion of the sentence that is consistent with the new edge's structure. :type lhs: Nonterminal :param lhs: The new edge's left-hand side, specifying the hypothesized tree's node value. :type rhs: list(Nonterminal and str) :param rhs: The new edge's right-hand side, specifying the hypothesized tree's children. :type dot: int :param dot: The position of the new edge's dot. This position specifies what prefix of the production's right hand side is consistent with the text. In particular, if ``sentence`` is the list of tokens in the sentence, then ``okens[span[0]:span[1]]`` can be spanned by the children specified by ``rhs[:dot]``.
