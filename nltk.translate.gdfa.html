This module symmetrisatizes the source-to-target and target-to-source word alignment output and produces, aka. GDFA algorithm (Koehn, 2005). Step 1: Find the intersection of the bidirectional alignment. Step 2: Search for additional neighbor alignment points to be added, given these criteria: (i) neighbor alignments points are not in the intersection and (ii) neighbor alignments are in the union.
