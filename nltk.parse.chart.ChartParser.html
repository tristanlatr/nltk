View In Hierarchy

A generic chart parser. A "strategy", or list of ``ChartRuleI`` instances, is used to decide what edges to add to the chart. In particular, ``ChartParser`` uses the following algorithm to parse texts: | Until no new edges are added: | For each *rule* in *strategy*: | Apply *rule* to any applicable edges in the chart. | Return any complete parses in the chart
