A cached version of ``TopDownPredictRule``. After the first time this rule is applied to an edge with a given ``end`` and ``next``, it will not generate any more edges for edges with that ``end`` and ``next``. If ``chart`` or ``grammar`` are changed, then the cache is flushed.
