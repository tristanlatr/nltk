Default preprocessing for a sequence of sentences. Creates two iterators: - sentences padded and turned into sequences of `nltk.util.everygrams` - sentences padded as above and chained together for a flat stream of words :param order: Largest ngram length produced by `everygrams`. :param text: Text to iterate over.
