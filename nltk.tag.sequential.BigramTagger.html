A tagger that chooses a token's tag based its word string and on the preceding words' tag. In particular, a tuple consisting of the previous tag and the word is looked up in a table, and the corresponding tag is returned. :param train: The corpus of training data, a list of tagged sentences :type train: list(list(tuple(str, str))) :param model: The tagger model :type model: dict :param backoff: Another tagger which this tagger will consult when it is unable to tag a word :type backoff: TaggerI :param cutoff: The number of instances of training data the tagger must see in order not to use the backoff tagger :type cutoff: int
