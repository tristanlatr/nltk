class StackWidget(CanvasWidget): (source)

View In Hierarchy

A canvas widget that keeps a list of canvas widgets in a vertical line. Attributes: - ``align``: The horizontal alignment of the children. Possible values are ``'left'``, ``'center'``, and ``'right'``. By default, children are center-aligned. - ``space``: The amount of vertical space to place between children. By default, one pixel of space is used. - ``ordered``: If true, then keep the children in their original order.
