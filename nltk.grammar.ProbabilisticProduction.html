class ProbabilisticProduction(Production, ImmutableProbabilisticMixIn): (source)

View In Hierarchy

A probabilistic context free grammar production. A PCFG ``ProbabilisticProduction`` is essentially just a ``Production`` that has an associated probability, which represents how likely it is that this production will be used. In particular, the probability of a ``ProbabilisticProduction`` records the likelihood that its right-hand side is the correct instantiation for any given occurrence of its left-hand side. :see: ``Production``
