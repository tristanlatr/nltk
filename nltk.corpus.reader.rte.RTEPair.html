class RTEPair(object): (source)

View In Hierarchy

Container for RTE text-hypothesis pairs. The entailment relation is signalled by the ``value`` attribute in RTE1, and by ``entailment`` in RTE2 and RTE3. These both get mapped on to the ``entailment`` attribute of this class.
