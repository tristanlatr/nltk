nltk.misc.chomsky

module documentation
(source)

CHOMSKY is an aid to writing linguistic papers in the style of the great master. It is based on selected phrases taken from actual books and articles written by Noam Chomsky. Upon request, it assembles the phrases in the elegant stylistic patterns that Chomsky is noted for. To generate n sentences of linguistic wisdom, type (CHOMSKY n) -- for example (CHOMSKY 5) generates half a screen of linguistic truth.
