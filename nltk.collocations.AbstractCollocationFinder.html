View In Hierarchy

An abstract base class for collocation finders whose purpose is to collect collocation candidate frequencies, filter and rank them. As a minimum, collocation finders require the frequencies of each word in a corpus, and the joint frequency of word tuples. This data should be provided through nltk.probability.FreqDist objects or an identical interface.
